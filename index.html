Life can get messy. Relationships get complicated. Work and life’s demands weigh heavily. Whether you’re looking to better navigate your personal challenges, or grow deeper in your connections with the people you love, you’re in the right place. Full Bloom Counseling Group is a group private practice of experienced, dedicated therapists who take their work seriously — and who will take yours seriously too.

We offer both in-person sessions in Denver, Colorado and online therapy for clients across the state.

Why Full Bloom

A Practice Built on Belonging

We believe therapy is not about fixing you — it’s about helping you come home to yourself. Whether you need individual support, couples counseling, or specialized care for disordered eating — you’ll find a therapist here who truly gets it.

Our clinicians bring deep expertise in trauma healing, Enneagram-based growth, body liberation, EMDR, the Safe and Sound Protocol, and more — all grounded in warmth, respect, and clinical excellence.

Our Practice

A Group of Therapists Who Care Deeply

Full Bloom Counseling was founded by Becca Moravec with a vision for a practice where therapists genuinely love their work — and clients feel that in every session.

We’re a tight-knit team of clinicians who consult with each other, hold each other accountable, and show up fully for every client. We see clients from Denver, Boulder, Colorado Springs, Fort Collins, and throughout Colorado via telehealth.
