Clinical Associate

Natalie Siegel

LPC, Certified Intuitive Eating Counselor

Being human isn't always easy, and no one should have to go through life's challenges alone. Every person deserves a space to process complex feelings and emotions — free of any judgment or bias.

Enter me, your therapist and teammate. I bring empathy, humor, and professional experience. You bring yourself and the unique expertise on your life. Together, we'll work as a team to achieve your therapeutic goals.

As your therapist, I promise to create a safe and welcoming space for you to come as you are—a place where you can grow and blossom into the most authentic version of yourself.

When I'm not working, I'm usually baking cookies in my kitchen, enjoying the beautiful Colorado outdoors, petting every dog I meet, or trying a new local restaurant with my partner.

“The curious paradox is that when I accept myself just as I am, then I can change.” — Carl Rogers
