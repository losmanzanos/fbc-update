Pre-Marital Counseling

Pre-marital counseling isn't about proving your relationship is ready. It's about making sure you actually know each other — your conflict styles, your money habits, your family-of-origin stories, your assumptions about what a partnership should look like. Most couples discover things in this process they're glad they found out before the wedding.

Pre-marital & pre-engagement counseling — Denver & telehealth across Colorado

Build a Strong Foundation

At Full Bloom Counseling, our pre-marital program is built around the Prepare-Enrich assessment — a research-backed tool used by over 4 million couples worldwide. It identifies your relationship's strengths and growth areas across communication, conflict, finances, intimacy, family expectations, and more.

This isn't therapy for broken things. It's investment in something that matters. Couples who complete pre-marital counseling report higher satisfaction, lower divorce rates, and better conflict resolution skills years later.

Pre-marital & pre-engagement counseling — Denver & telehealth across Colorado

Why Choose Pre-Marital Counseling?

A thriving marriage doesn't just happen — it's built. Our structured 9-session program is designed to help you and your partner:

✦

Improve communication and conflict resolution skills

✦

Deepen emotional and physical intimacy

✦

Align on finances, family planning, and future goals

✦

Understand each other's values, expectations, and family history

Prepare-Enrich: A Personalized Approach

We use the Prepare-Enrich assessment — a trusted, research-backed tool used worldwide. Both partners complete the assessment independently, and your results are combined into a personalized roadmap that highlights your strengths and surfaces areas to work on together.

Whether you're newly engaged, considering a commitment, or planning a ceremony years away, this process meets you where you are.

"We thought we knew each other so well. The assessment surfaced patterns we didn't even know we had — and gave us a language to talk about them without it becoming an argument."

— Couple, engaged, Denver

Program Details & Investment

Our 9-session pre-marital program includes the full Prepare-Enrich assessment for both partners, three assessment review sessions, and six skill-building sessions focused on your specific growth areas. Sessions are typically 50–60 minutes and available weekly or bi-weekly to fit your timeline.

Available in-person in Denver and via telehealth across Colorado. Reach out to discuss scheduling and fees.
