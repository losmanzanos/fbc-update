Know Yourself Deeply

Enneagram Therapy

The Enneagram is a powerful tool for personal growth and self-awareness. At Full Bloom Counseling, we integrate this transformative framework into our therapeutic approach to help individuals understand their core motivations, strengths, and challenges. Whether you are new to the Enneagram or looking to deepen your knowledge, our certified Enneagram teachers and practitioners are here to guide you on your journey.

What is the Enneagram?

The Enneagram is a system of nine interconnected personality types. It reveals how we relate to the world, other people, and ourselves. Each type has its own unique characteristics, and understanding these traits can help you unlock your potential, heal emotional wounds, and improve your relationships.

Check out the Narrative Enneagram for more information on each type.

Why the Enneagram Matters for Personal Growth

At Full Bloom Counseling, we believe the Enneagram is a profound tool for growth. By understanding your Enneagram type, you gain insight into your unconscious patterns and motivations. This awareness can lead to greater emotional balance, improved relationships, and a deeper sense of purpose.

Our therapists are trained in the Narrative Enneagram approach, which emphasizes personal narrative and transformation through understanding your type’s core motivations and how they show up in your life.

Enneagram Workshops and Classes

Full Bloom Counseling offers specialized Enneagram workshops and classes for individuals and groups. Our certified Enneagram teachers lead these interactive sessions, offering valuable tools for self-discovery, conflict resolution, and personal development.

Our work together may include: Intro to the Enneagram: Understanding Your Type, Deepening Your Enneagram Journey: Advanced Techniques for Transformation, The Enneagram and Relationships: How Understanding Your Type Can Strengthen Your Bonds.

Enneagram and Therapy: A Holistic Approach

Integrating the Enneagram with therapy provides a deeper, more comprehensive approach to personal healing. Our certified Enneagram practitioners work alongside clients to explore their type’s influence on their life experiences, from childhood wounds to current struggles. With the Enneagram’s support, clients gain a clearer path toward emotional healing, resilience, and a balanced life.

Certified Enneagram Teachers and Practitioners at Full Bloom Counseling

At Full Bloom Counseling, we pride ourselves on our team’s extensive training in the Narrative Enneagram. Our therapists are not just trained in general Enneagram practices—they are certified teachers and practitioners, bringing a depth of expertise and authenticity to the healing process.
