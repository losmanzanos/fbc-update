Everything you need to know about therapy at Full Bloom Counseling.

We are out-of-network providers and do not bill insurance directly. However, many clients receive significant reimbursement through their out-of-network mental health benefits. Here’s how it works:

  1. Call your insurance and ask: “What are my out-of-network mental health benefits?”
  2. We provide a Superbill (itemized receipt) after each session.
  3. Submit the Superbill to your insurance for reimbursement — many clients receive 40–80% back.
